About online speed dating

What is speed dating? + −

Speed ​​dating is a short event where you can meet single people near you. You are typically grouped with people within a similar age to yourself.

Speed dating events usually last around an hour, and you’ll have short dates with between 10-20 people in that hour.

Traditionally the venue is a bar, however online speed dating is becoming very popular, where you meet people through short video calls.

How does online speed dating work? + −

When you join an online speed dating event, you’ll video date different people for between 3-5 minutes each person.

If you liked the person you were talking to, you can 'Yes' that person after the date. If you and your date both 'Yes' each other, you’ll match and your contact details will be exchanged after the event.

Why should you participate in a speed dating event? + −

Speed dating is a great way to meet different people near you. The dates are short so you’ll get the chance to meet lots of different people at each event, in a safe and controlled environment.

Why is Copper better than traditional online dating apps? + −

Many people have told us that they are tired of traditional dating apps such as Meetic, Tinder, Match and Bumble.

They waste time swiping and messaging, when it ultimately leads nowhere. This process can be both time consuming and emotionally draining.

With traditional apps, the swiping process can lead to heightened expectations which usually then fall short. Often the chat fizzles out or, for various reasons, there is no chemistry in real life.

With video dating, you’ll have the opportunity to see if there is genuine rapport and chemistry before arranging a date in real-life. Research shows that this approach leads to a much higher success rate for in-person dates, as the initial video dates answer many initial questions and concerns in advance.

Tips for video dating

Setup and technology + −

For a setup to make you look your best, follow these tips:

  • Keep the camera at eye level
  • Ensure the main light source is in front of you
  • Wear plain coloured clothes, as lines and patterns don’t show well on video
  • Test your audio and video before you start dating
Body language and eye contact + −

Follow these body language and eye contact tips while on a video date:

  • Sit up straight and don’t slouch
  • Use hand gestures when emphasising what you are saying
  • Look at the camera at points throughout your date, as this gives the impression of looking into their eyes
What should you ask in a speed dating event? + −

Keep in mind that each date will be short. Typical date topics revolve around jobs and where you’re living, so try and mix it up a little with more interesting and original questions. Don´t forget to actively listen to the other person.

Remember that even though you’re on a video call, body language, smiling and speaking clarity are hugely important and will make you appear more attractive to the person you are talking to.
