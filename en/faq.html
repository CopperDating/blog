Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost? + −

Copper is completely free to use. Just join an event and start dating people near you!

How do I sign up for Copper? + −

There is no need to register for an event before it starts. When the event starts, a button will appear on the home page to join the event. After pressing this button, you will need to enter basic information about yourself before joining the event. This information is the only required registration information.

On what days and at what time are the Copper events? + −

Please visit our events page for the latest information on the time and dates of our events.

Do I need to download something? + −

You do not need any additional software to run Copper Dating. The speed dating event runs in your browser, such as Chrome or Microsoft Edge.

Why is Copper better than Zoom? + −

Copper's platform is fully automated, taking care of organizing the matching of users and the timing of appointments. Participants have told us that because of this, the experience is much smoother than dating through Zoom.

In addition, our platform is able to grow easily so that you can be part of large events without any inconvenience.
