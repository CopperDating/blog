How does Copper work?

At 8pm (Spanish time) every Thursday and Sunday, this site becomes a video dating platform, where you can date different people for 3 minutes each. The event lasts for 60 minutes and you can have unlimited video dates during this time.

After each date, you have the option to ‘Yes’ that person. If you both ‘Yes’ each other, your contact details are exchanged. You then have the option to continue dating other people, or end the event.

Why use Copper?

Copper is simple and reliable to use. You don't need to download an app or complete a long registration form. We only ask for basic sign-up information before you start dating, such as your name, email and age.

Why do people love video speed dating?

It allows you to have authentic, quick and genuine conversations. No swiping, no boring messages, no edited pictures. Get to know real people and find out if there is any chemistry.

How do I register?

Before the event on Thursday or Sunday, enter your email address at the top of this page. That will secure your place at the event. All you need to do after this is turn up here on Thursday or Sunday at 8pm!

Copper security and privacy

Copper Dating is a safe and secure platform. Only your contact details are shared with your matches. We also delete all attendees' personal information from our database 48 hours after the event.
